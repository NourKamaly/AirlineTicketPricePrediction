Features
1. Date: The date of the journey

2. Airline: The name of the airline

3. Ch code: the airline code

4. Num code: The number which describes the sequence of journeys.

5. Dep time: The time when the journey starts from the source.

6. Time taken: Total duration of the flight.

7. Total stops: Total stops between the source and destination.

8. Arrival time: Time of arrival at the destination.

9. Type: wether the flight was in economy or business class 

10. Route: This contains information on starting and ending location of the journey in the standard format used by airlines.
       . Source: The source from which the service begins.
       . Destination: The destination where the service ends.


Target
Price: The price of the ticket
Category : wether the ticket is considered cheap,moderate, expensive or very expensive
