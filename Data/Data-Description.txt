Features
1 . Date: The date of the journey

2 . Airline: The name of the airline

3 . Ch-Code: the airline code

4 . Dep_Time: The time when the journey starts from the source.

5 . Time_Taken: Total duration of the flight.

6 . Total_Stops: Total stops between the source and destination.

7 . Arrival_Time: Time of arrival at the destination.

8 . Route: This contains information on starting and ending location of the journey in the standard format used by airlines.

	. Source: The source from which the service begins.
      . Destination: The destination where the service ends.

9 . Num_Code: The number which describes the sequence of journeys.

10. Type: ...

Target
Price: The price of the ticket
